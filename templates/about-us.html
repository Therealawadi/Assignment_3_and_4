WHY WE COLLECT DATA

As an educational institution, we collect student data to ensure that we can provide the best possible learning experience and support to our students. This data helps us understand the diverse needs of our student body, tailor our educational programs and services to meet those needs, and continuously improve our offerings over time. By gathering information such as academic performance, demographic details, and feedback from students, we can identify areas for enhancement, implement targeted interventions, and measure the effectiveness of our initiatives. Additionally, student data enables us to comply with legal and regulatory requirements, maintain accurate records, and safeguard the welfare and security of our students. Ultimately, our goal in collecting student data is to empower our students to succeed academically, professionally, and personally, while fostering a safe, inclusive, and supportive learning environment.
